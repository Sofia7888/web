Itinerant Office
Branding
We are honored to present our work in creating the visual identity for Itinerant Office, a prominent architecture festival that garners international attention. This event brings together professionals, studios, and visionaries from the world of architecture from various countries to discuss the challenges and opportunities in the field of global architecture. The primary goal of this project has been to reflect the diversity and interconnectedness of the global architectural community that gathers at the Itinerant Office festival. The visual identity we've developed captures the essence of collaboration, innovation, and exploration that are central to the world of architecture. We've worked on designing visual elements, from the logo to the color palette and typography, that embody the internationality and collaborative spirit of the event. Our work goes beyond creating a mere identity; we've built a visual experience that conveys Itinerant Office's dedication to global architecture. Discover how we have contributed to defining the image and character of Itinerant Office as an international festival that unites creative minds from around the world to drive architecture toward a better future. More than just an event, Itinerant Office is a beacon of global architecture and intercultural exchange.
